Who We Are

At Interactive Investigative Solutions, we have diverse investigative backgrounds in both criminal & civil matters which allow us to offer services in a multitude of areas.

We offer unparalleled expertise in Florida Workers' Compensation Compliance, with many of our investigators having previously worked for the Florida Department of Financial Services.

We have provided personal security for the chairman & senior level executives of a Fortune 500 company and are proficient in Threat & Risk Assessment.

We are located in Sarasota on Florida's Gulf Coast, but the strength of our network means we can accept contract work anywhere in the world, domestic or abroad.
